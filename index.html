Our Mission

Welcome to the Cherubim & Seraphim Ministers Biography project. Our mission is to preserve and share the inspiring stories of dedicated ministers who have served in the C&S Church. Through these biographies, we honor their contributions, celebrate their achievements, and inspire future generations of ministers.
