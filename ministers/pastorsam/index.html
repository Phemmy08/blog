Pastor Sam Makanju

Pastor Lead Pastor · Dayspring Seraphic Church

Biography

Pastor Sam has been serving as the Senior Pastor at Dayspring Seraphic Church for over 4 years. He holds a Master's degree in Agricultural Extension and is passionate about sharing the gospel and mentoring the next generation of leaders.

Under his guidance, the church has expanded its outreach programs and established a strong community presence, touching hundreds of lives across the region through dedicated ministry and service.

"The greatest ministry is the ministry of love and service to God's people."

— Pastor Sam Makanju
