Pastor Segun Michael

Pastor Apostolic Shepherd · Word of Life Reconciliation Outreach (WRO)

Biography

Pastor Segun Michael serves as the Apostolic Shepherd of the Word of Life Reconciliation Outreach (WRO), a non-denominational ministry bearing a divine mandate at its very heart: reconciling men back to God. The strategy for achieving this mandate is built on three pillars — a correct life, a correct message (Jesus), and a correct methodology (Love).

A devoted preacher of the gospel of Christ, Pastor Segun Michael is recognised as one of the frontiers of the revival labour amidst the Aladura movement, carrying the torch of genuine faith and renewal within a landscape hungry for authentic Christianity.

Each December, he hosts an annual general retreat, a gathering designed to give further expression to the ministry's vision on the landscape — bringing believers together for deeper encounter, renewed commitment, and alignment with the purpose of God.

"A correct life, carrying a correct message — Jesus — via a correct methodology: Love."

— The mandate of Word of Life Reconciliation Outreach

Pastor Segun Michael is happily married and blessed with children.
