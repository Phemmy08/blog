Biography

Pastor Isaac Shola Ajewole is the Lead Pastor of The Revival Centre of Cherubim and Seraphim Movement Church, Omu-Aran. Known warmly as Daddy Ajewole, he is a sound teacher of God's word and a minister deeply committed to the work of discipleship — building believers who are rooted, grounded, and mature in Christ.

He stands as one of the major frontliners in the revival labour within the C&S church, consistently championing the cause of genuine New Testament Christianity within the Aladura tradition. His ministry is marked by a love for God's word and a passion to see the body of Christ grow beyond form and tradition into the fullness of what God has ordained.

"A minister committed to discipleship — building children both spiritual and physical."

— The heart of Pastor Isaac Shola Ajewole's ministry

Daddy Ajewole is happily married and blessed with children — both spiritual and physical — a testament to a life poured out in faithful service to God and His people.
