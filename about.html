Honoring the Legacy of Cherubim & Seraphim Ministers

History of the Cherubim & Seraphim Church

The Cherubim & Seraphim (C&S) Church is a spiritual Christian movement founded in Nigeria in 1925. Known for its deep roots in prayer, worship, and prophetic ministry, the C&S Church has grown into a global fellowship, impacting millions through its message of holiness, peace, and service to God and humanity.

Purpose of This Site

This website is dedicated to preserving and sharing the biographies of ministers who have served faithfully in the C&S Church. By documenting their stories, we aim to inspire future generations, celebrate the legacy of faith, and provide a resource for the global C&S community and beyond.
